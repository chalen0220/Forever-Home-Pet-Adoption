About Us

We Make it Our Life Mission to Save Animal's Lives.

Saving Animals Since 2000

Forever Home Pet Adoption Agency was founded in 2000 by Jack Jacob. As a nonprofit organization, We exist to rescue abused, neglected, and homeless animals all around the country.

We Love and Respect Our Family

FHPA has a great community of dedicated staff and volunteers who give their time to care for our animals. We truly believe that if we love and respect these animals like family, they will live much happier, fufilling lives.
